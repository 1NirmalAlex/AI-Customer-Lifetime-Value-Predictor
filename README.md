### 🚀 Unleash the Power of Data-Driven Business Decisions
In the modern E-commerce landscape, knowing the future value of your customer is the key to profitability. This project features a **Machine Learning-powered analytical engine** that predicts how much a customer is likely to spend over their lifetime using their historical behavior.

---

## 🧠 Core Intelligence: The RFM Engine
This system doesn't just guess; it analyzes **RFM** metrics—the gold standard of customer analytics:
* **Recency (R):** How long since the last purchase?
* **Frequency (F):** How often do they buy?
* **Monetary (M):** What is their average spend per order?

Using a **Linear Regression** model with over **90% accuracy**, the AI maps these patterns to predict a future dollar value for every individual customer.

---

## 🎨 Interactive Dashboard Experience
We have deployed a high-end, futuristic **Streamlit Web Application** that allows business owners to:
- ⚡ **Real-time Prediction:** Input customer metrics and get an instant CLV estimate.
- 📊 **Visual Analytics:** View customer "Health" via an interactive Gauge chart.
